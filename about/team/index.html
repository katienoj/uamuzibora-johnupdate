Uamuzi Bora is made up of a diverse team of clinicians, developers and educators from Kenya, the UK and Norway.

Kenya Team

The Kenya team work with the Ministry of Health and partners to support mobile information technology and EMR implementation, education and training.

John Haskew

Director

John is a medical doctor who specialises in Public Health. He founded Uamuzi Bora in 2009 and currently lives Kenya.
