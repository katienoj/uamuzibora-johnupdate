A diverse set of individuals make up Uamuzi Bora from locations such as Kenya, the UK and Norway. Whether they're involved as employees or as volunteers, we work seemlessly from different continents to make a difference.

Clinical Team

Our clinical team work directly in HIV and MCH clinics in Western Kenya, liasing with both clinicians and medical records staff to help enter data and use the EMR system effectively.
