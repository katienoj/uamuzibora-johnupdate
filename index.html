About us

We are a UK-registered charity who use mobile information technology and an electronic medical record to improve public health and primary care in Western Kenya.

Uamuzi Bora means “a better choice” in Luhya, a local language spoken in Western Kenya, and was chosen by the local communities to describe the work we do.

Read our story »

Projects

We support the Ministry of Health and partners in Kenya to implement an electronic medical record to improve linkage to care, clinical decision support, adherence to treatment and retention in care.

We currently implement an electronic medical record for HIV and Maternal and Child Health in Western Kenya with the Ministry of Health.
