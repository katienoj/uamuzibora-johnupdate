About us

We are a UK-registered charity working in Western Kenya to support the national health system provide care and support to people living with HIV by providing an electronic medical records (EMR) system.

Uamuzi Bora means “a better choice” in Luhya, a local language spoken in Western Kenya, and was chosen by the local communities to describe the work we do.

Our data & code

We use an adapted version of OpenMRS as the main component of our EMR which we have extended with custom Python applications to verify our data set and produce anonymised aggregate data for use by the Ministry of Health.

We're keen to share the epidemiological data we produce and our source code.
